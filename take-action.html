Texas House Natural Resources Committee

The committee is hearing testimony on data center water use and conservation. Texas residents can submit written comments online without testifying in person, and these become part of the official record.

Texas Senate Finance Committee · Monday, July 27, 2026, 10:30 AM

The committee is studying the cost of the data center sales tax exemption and what safeguards Texans should get in return. Unlike the House, the Senate does not accept comments online: you have to register your position or testimony in person at the Capitol, either at a kiosk outside the hearing room or on a laptop connected to the Capitol's public WiFi, on the day of the hearing.
